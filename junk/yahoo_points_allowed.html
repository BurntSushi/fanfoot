    Defensive points allowed in Fantasy Football

    ID: SLN6441
    Refers to: Fantasy Football

    Description

    This article lists all the situations that count against the Points Allowed category in leagues using Team Defenses, and why the Points Allowed category may be different from the final score from an NFL game.


    Resolution

    In Yahoo! Sports Fantasy Football, scoring for the Points Allowed category in leagues using Team Defenses includes the following situations:

    • All TDs scored against the defense
    • All FGs scored by the opponent
    • All PATs (Extra Points and 2-PT conversions)
    • Special Teams Scoring
      • Kickoff returns for touchdowns
      • Punt returns for touchdowns
      • Blocked punts returned for touchdowns
    • All other scoring categories chosen within the league

    Please note: Points scored against the team's offensive unit are not counted in the Points Allowed category:

    • Interception returns for touchdowns
    • Fumble returns for touchdowns
    • Blocked field goals returned for touchdowns
    • Safeties
